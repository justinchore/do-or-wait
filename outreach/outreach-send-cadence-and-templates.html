Outreach — Send Cadence & Second-Touch Template

Cold outreach from your Outlook · review, drip, log, one value-led nudge

Daily send target

WhenVolume
Week 1About 15 to 20 a day, to warm up your sending.
Week 2 onwardRamp to about 30 to 40 a day if replies come and nothing bounces or flags.
CapAround 40 a day for cold from a personal mailbox.
Best windowsMid-morning Tuesday to Thursday. Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons.

Keep it plain text with minimal links. If bounces or spam complaints rise, pull back.

Process

  1. Review each draft before sending, and fix any forced or fake personalization opener.
  2. Send in the daily batch, and mark Status in the Outreach modal as you go.
  3. Second touch about 5 to 7 days later, sent as a reply to your own original email (same thread).
  4. When someone replies, move them into Leads and work the Do/Wait pipeline.
  5. Never say "just following up" or "circling back." Always lead with value.
