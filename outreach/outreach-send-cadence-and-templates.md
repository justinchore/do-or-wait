# Outreach — Send Cadence & Second-Touch Template

## Daily send target (cold, from your Outlook)
- Week 1: ~15–20/day (warm up the sending).
- Week 2+: ramp to ~30–40/day if replies come and nothing bounces/flags.
- Cap ~40/day for cold from a personal mailbox.
- Best windows: mid-morning Tue–Thu. Avoid Mon AM / Fri PM.
- Plain text, minimal links. If bounces or spam complaints rise, pull back.

## Process
1. Review each draft before sending — fix any fake/forced personalization opener.
2. Send in the daily batch; mark Status in the Outreach modal as you go.
3. Second touch ~5–7 days later, as a REPLY to your own original email (same thread).
4. When someone replies, move them into Leads and work the Do/Wait pipeline.
5. Never "just following up" / "circling back" — always lead with value.

## Swap-in value hooks (use whichever is true that week)
- Nearby unit just opened: "A space just opened near you that fits how [Company]'s volume moves. It's [size / city], flexible month-to-month."
- Seasonal / container overflow (great for fashion district): "Heading into peak season. If a container lands and your back room can't hold it, we've got flexible overflow space nearby you can take month-to-month."
- Cost / flexibility: "Quick one. Our all-in pricing usually runs about 20% to 30% under the all-in cost of a traditional space once you add CAM/NNN, with no long commitment."

Rules: about 90 to 120 words, no dashes, no bulleted lists in the email, sign "Best, Justin".
